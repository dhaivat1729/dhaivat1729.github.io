I work as a Machine learning research engineer at Samsung AI centre - Toronto. My recent experience involved extensively working with LLMs and AI agents. I worked on parameter efficient fine-tuning Llama3 using LoRA, as well as experience with prompt engineering for dataset creation. I have also worked on scripting torch models for inference on device, as well as worked with Quantization of LLMs for efficient inference. I am currently interested in exploring new opportunities in the domain of Generative AI, specifically AI agents. Feel free to reach out to me either on email or twitter for any collaboration or new opportunities.

Before that I was a research masters student at MILA, University of Montreal, under supervision of Professor Liam Paull, where I mainly worked on Probabilistic object detection. I worked on trustworthy uncertainty estimation for my thesis, which got accepted to ICRA 2022. I also worked on identifying flaws of existing set of probabilistic object detectors, which got published as a position paper at ICML 2020. Before coming to MILA, I did MS in Robotics with Prof Madhav Krishna, and worked on many things including self driving cars and autonomous drones.
